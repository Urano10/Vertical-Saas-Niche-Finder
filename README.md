# Vertical SaaS Niche Finder

An interactive quiz designed to help readers explore potential **Vertical SaaS ideas** based on their answers.

This is an inspiration and discovery tool, not professional business advice.

---

## What This Quiz Does

- Asks a series of multiple-choice questions
- Generates personalized SaaS project suggestions based on your answers
- Works instantly in the browser with zero lag
- Does not require sign-up, login, or registration

---

## Privacy & Data

**This quiz is fully GDPR-compliant:**

- ✅ No personal data collected or requested
- ✅ No email addresses, names, or personal information needed
- ✅ No cookies, tracking, or analytics
- ✅ No data sent to any server
- ✅ All logic runs entirely in your browser
- ✅ Can be used offline with full privacy protection

Your answers exist only on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any information.

## How to Use

You can:
- Open the quiz directly in your browser
- Download the repository and run it locally
- Use it as inspiration or adapt it for personal experiments
